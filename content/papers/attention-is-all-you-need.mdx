## Key Innovation

The Transformer architecture introduced a novel approach to sequence modeling that relied entirely on attention mechanisms, eliminating the need for recurrent or convolutional layers. This enabled significantly better parallelization and led to state-of-the-art results in machine translation.

## Impact

This paper fundamentally changed the landscape of deep learning, becoming the foundation for modern large language models including GPT, BERT, and their successors.
