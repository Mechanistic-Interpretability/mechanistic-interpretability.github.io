## Dataset Construction

ImageNet was constructed by using Amazon Mechanical Turk to clean and verify the quality of images. The hierarchical structure follows WordNet synsets, providing semantic relationships between categories.

## Impact

ImageNet became the de facto benchmark for object recognition and the ImageNet Large Scale Visual Recognition Challenge (ILSVRC) drove major advances in deep learning for computer vision.
