## Key Innovation

BERT introduced masked language modeling, where random tokens are masked and the model learns to predict them using bidirectional context. This differs from previous left-to-right or right-to-left language models.

## Architecture

Two model sizes were released:

-   BERT-Base: 12 layers, 768 hidden units, 12 attention heads (110M parameters)
-   BERT-Large: 24 layers, 1024 hidden units, 16 attention heads (340M parameters)
