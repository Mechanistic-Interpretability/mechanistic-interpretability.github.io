## Chain-of-Thought Prompting

The key finding is that simply adding "Let's think step by step" before an answer prompt significantly improves performance on reasoning tasks. This works even for models not explicitly trained on reasoning data.

## Results

Zero-shot-CoT outperformed zero-shot LLM performance on diverse reasoning benchmarks including arithmetic, symbolic reasoning, and commonsense reasoning.
