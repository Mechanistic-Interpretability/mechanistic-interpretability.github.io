## Impact

The paper introduces the key vocabulary and decomposition tools of the field: the residual stream as a bandwidth-limited communication bus, attention heads as independent low-rank linear operations, and the QK/OV circuit factorization. It argues that transformers possess enormous linear structure and that most of their behavior can be understood by tracing matrix multiplications through the model.

These relationships enabled better resource allocation for training large models.

## Implications

This is the founding document of mechanistic interpretability. Every subsequent paper in the Transformer Circuits thread — and most circuit-level work outside Anthropic — builds directly on this framework. It is the first paper to read before entering the field.
