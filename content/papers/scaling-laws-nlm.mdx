## Power Law Relationships

The paper established predictable scaling relationships between:

- Model parameters (N)
- Dataset size (D)
- Compute budget (C)
- Final loss (L)

These relationships enabled better resource allocation for training large models.

## Implications

Understanding these scaling laws has become crucial for planning large-scale training runs and predicting model capabilities at different scales.
